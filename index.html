Who Am I?

My name is Shreyas Chaturvedi. I am 14 years old and currently studying in Class 9H at CMS Gomti Nagar 1 Campus, Lucknow. I am a dedicated student who loves learning, creativity, leadership, technology, and innovation. I enjoy participating in school activities and building creative digital ideas.

Achievements

I was awarded Student of the Year in the Junior Section at City Montessori School. I also served as the Tech Head in my school council team, where I contributed through leadership, digital creativity, teamwork, and technology-based ideas. My journey reflects hard work, discipline, confidence, and consistency.

Interests

I deeply enjoy coding, web development, futuristic UI designing, technology projects, educational creativity, photography, leadership activities, and making visually beautiful digital experiences. I am always excited to learn advanced concepts and create innovative digital projects.
